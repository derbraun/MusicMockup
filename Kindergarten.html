Kindergarten

The Trimester System
The school year is divided into three trimesters. The students will receive their report cards
at the end of each trimester. During the trimester, the students will attend four weeks each
of all three specialties:  computers, P.E., and music. They will attend once a week. The order
in which they will attend the specialties will depend upon which rotation they are in. Please
consult with your child's core teacher to determine the rotation schedule. The following is
the general curriculum for each of the trimesters.
Trimester One (4 weeks)

○
Learn the difference between a speaking voice and a singing voice.

○
Echo singing.

○
Learn about beat and rhythm.

Trimester Two (4 weeks)

○
Learn the difference between a speaking voice and a singing voice.

○
Echo singing.

○
Practice hearing the beat and the rhythm.

○
Introduced to quarter note beat (horse) and eighth note beat (hippo). See the
relationship between the two.

Trimester Three (4 weeks)

○
Review the difference between a speaking voice and a singing voice.

○
Echo singing.

○
Holding the tune in your head.

○
Creating new songs and chants using beat and rhythm.

○
Practice hearing the beat.

○
Practice the beat (horse) and the divided beat (hippo)

○
Sing in so-mi tones.
