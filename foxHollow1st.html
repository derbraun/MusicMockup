First Grade

The Trimester System
The school year is divided into three trimesters. The students will receive their report cards
at the end of each trimester. During the trimester, the students will attend four weeks each
of all three specialties:  computers, P.E., and music. The order in which they will attend the
specialties will depend upon which rotation they are in. Please consult with your child's core
teacher to determine the rotation schedule.

Music follows the Utah State Core Curriculum for music. Therefore, the elements of music are
introduced. The following is the general curriculum for each of the trimesters.
Trimester One (4 weeks)

○
Learn about high and low pitch.

○
Learn about dynamics (p and f).

○
Learn about tempo (largo and allegro).

○
Learn solfege (so, mi).

○
Learn music notation for simple so-mi songs.

○
Practice beat and rhythm.

○
Learn about the quarter note, eighth note, and sixteenth note.

Trimester Two (4 weeks)

○
Learn articulation (staccato, legato, and accent).

○
Learn about a fermata and a ritard.

○
Learn about the half note, whole note, and treble clef.

○
Learn solfege (so, mi).

○
Learn music notation for simple so-mi songs.

○
Practice beat and rhythm.

Trimester Three (4 weeks)

○
Music Appreciation: Learn about classical music, jazz, and musical theater.

○
Learn about the base clef and the staff.

○
Learn solfege (so, mi, la).

○
Learn music notation for simple so-mi and so-mi-la songs.

○
Practice articulation exercises.

○
Practice beat and rhythm.

○
Practice sight singing so-mi songs.
